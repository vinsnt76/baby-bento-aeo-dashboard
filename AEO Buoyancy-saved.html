Buoyancy Report - December 2025 Performance Review

This report analyzes website performance from November to December 2025, focusing on schema implementation impact. Schema adoption has driven positive momentum for product and blog content, leading to "The Lift" in key pages and queries. Despite a decline in broader 'Product snippets' search appearance, targeted schema application has captured new visibility and clicks. Some pages have entered "The Faded" category due to a complete loss of clicks or ranking post-schema implementation. Notably, no pages fell into "The Pivot" or "Retention" categories, indicating stark shifts rather than gradual changes or stable performance.

Appendix: Example HTML & JSON-LD for Schema-Enabled Content

Example 1: Australia Insulated Lunch Bags Top Brands 2026

Demonstrates integration of BlogPosting and FAQPage schema for a product review blog post.

  • HTML Structure: Includes standard HTML elements, a hero image, and sections for "Why Choose an Insulated Lunch Bag?", "Top Brands Reviewed for 2026," and "Frequently Asked Questions." Includes a "Blue Finding" and "Green Tip" paragraph.
  • JSON-LD: Contains @graph with BlogPosting and FAQPage types, detailing headline, description, image, author, publisher, datePublished, dateModified, and mainEntity for FAQs.

Blue Finding: High-quality product reviews benefit significantly from detailed Product and FAQ schema, enhancing visibility and user engagement, even if organic position fluctuates.

Green Tip: Regularly audit schema performance for individual pages. A/B test different schema types to identify the most impactful combinations for specific content formats.

Example 2: Lunchbox Solutions for Busy Parents

Illustrates implementation of BlogPosting, HowTo, and FAQPage schema for a guide on packing lunchboxes.

  • HTML Structure: Features standard HTML, a hero image, an introduction, a step-by-step "How to Pack a Healthy and Quick Lunchbox" section with numbered list items, and a "Frequently Asked Questions" section. Includes a "Blue Finding" and "Green Tip" paragraph.
  • JSON-LD: Contains @graph with BlogPosting, HowTo, and FAQPage types. The HowTo schema includes name, description, estimatedCost, supply, tool, and step details. The BlogPosting and FAQPage schemas provide similar details as in Example 1.

Blue Finding: How-to guides with clearly defined steps, tools, and supplies are excellent candidates for HowTo schema, dramatically improving visibility in instructional search features.

Green Tip: For practical guides, consider creating complementary content (e.g., product lists for tools/supplies) and interlinking to enhance topical authority and user journey.
